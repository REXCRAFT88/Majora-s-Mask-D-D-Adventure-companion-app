# Majora's Mask D\&D Adventure Companion

The Majora's Mask D\&D Adventure Companion is a comprehensive desktop companion application designed to enhance your experience with The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask D\&D Adventure Modul created by u/lemiel14n3.

## Features

* **PDF Viewer:** Seamlessly view the integrated "Majora's Mask Adventure Book" PDF, complete with a table of contents for easy navigation.
* **Character Management:** Keep track of all in-game characters, including their details and interactions. Features for viewing and editing character information are available.
* **Quest \& Questline Tracking:** A robust system for managing quests and questlines. View quest details, track your progress, and edit quest information as needed.
* **Curiosity Shop Management:** Explore and manage items found within the Curiosity Shop.
* **Timeline \& Time Controls:** Visualize and interact with the game's unique three-day cycle. Features include a time calculator and controls to help plan your adventures.
* **General Tracker View:** A versatile view to track various aspects of your game progression and collectibles.
* **Wiki Viewer:** Access an integrated wiki for in-depth information about Termina, its inhabitants, and its secrets.
* **Notification System:** Stay informed with timely notifications about important in-game events or personal objectives.
* **Custom Quest \& CharacterS** You can create custom characters and qwuestlines that automatically populate on the timeline
